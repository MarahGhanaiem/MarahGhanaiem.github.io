About Me

Marah Ghanaiem a Palestinian artist from the city of Baqa al-Gharbiyye. She is a fine arts student whose artistic practice spans between painting and sculpture. This diversity reflects her interest in various expressive mediums and her continuous exploration of new ideas and innovative techniques. She has been significantly influenced by the Palestinian society and the challenges it faces, which are reflected in her artistic works.
